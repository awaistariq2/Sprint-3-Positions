Demonstrating Position

This section is here to create enough content to make the page scrollable. As you scroll down, notice a few things:

The header at the very top has position: sticky. It scrolls with the content until it hits the top of the viewport, where it ‘sticks’ in place.

The profile card also has position: relative. By itself, this doesn’t do much, but it creates a positioning context for its child elements.

The red notification badge has position: absolute, which positions it relative to the boundaries of the profile card, its nearest ‘positioned’ ancestor.
